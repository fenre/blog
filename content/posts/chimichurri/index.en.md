A fresh, tangy herb sauce from Argentina, made for grilled meat. Easy to make, and far better than most bottled sauces.

### Ingredients:

  * 1 large bunch fresh flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped

  * A few sprigs fresh oregano, finely chopped (or 1 ts dried)

  * 3–4 cloves garlic, finely chopped or pressed

  * 1 red chili, finely chopped (or 1 ts chili flakes)

  * 3 tbs red wine vinegar

  * 1 dl olive oil

  * Salt and pepper

### Steps:

  * Mix parsley, oregano, garlic and chili in a bowl.

  * Stir in vinegar and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper.

  * Let the sauce rest for at least 10–15 minutes before serving, longer if you can.

  * Serve with grilled meat.
