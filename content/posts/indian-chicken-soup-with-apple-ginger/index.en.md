This is a recipe for a really good Indian chicken soup. It will be best if combined with a real chicken stock.

[![](images/img-001-ddfe3bac98.jpg)](images/img-002-d7032822f7.jpg)

### Ingredients:

  * 1 small onion, chopped

  * 2 cloves garlic, chopped

  * 1 red chili, chopped

  * 1 tbs fresh ginger, minced

  * 2 carrots, coarsely grated

  * 1 sour apple, coarsely grated

  * 2+ ts curry

  * 1 liter chicken stock

  * 2 dl coconut milk

  * 2-3 chicken fillets, in cubes

  * Salt and pepper

  * Fresh coriander

### Steps:

  * Sauté the onion in sunflower oil until soft and translucent.

  * Add garlic, chili and ginger. Let it sizzle for a minute, then add carrot, apple and curry. Stir well and add chicken stock.

  * Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 min.

  * Meanwhile, fry the chicken pieces in butter. Season with salt and pepper. The chicken does not need to be fully cooked through.

  * Add to the soup together with coconut milk. Bring to a boil and season to taste with curry, chili, salt and pepper.

  * Let the soup simmer for 10 min.

  * Add a handful of fresh coriander just before serving.

  * Serve with naan bread.
