I can rarely resist a really good salsa, and this is one of the best recipes I’ve come across. It goes with almost anything, and keeps for a few days in the fridge.

[![](images/img-001-876a2df7c1.jpg)](images/img-002-2ba4cb66c0.jpg)

### Ingredients:

  * 5–6 large, ripe, juicy tomatoes. Remove seeds and juice, and chop into small pieces.

  * 1/2 large red onion, finely chopped.

  * 1 green jalapeño. Remove seeds and chop finely.

  * A handful of chopped fresh coriander.

  * At least 2 cloves of garlic, pressed.

  * Juice of 2 limes. Salt and pepper.

### Method:

Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Squeeze lime over, and season to taste with salt. Enjoy!
