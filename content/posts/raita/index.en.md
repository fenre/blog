A cooling, tangy yoghurt sauce from Indian cooking — the perfect counterpoint to spiced food like tikka masala or curry. Quick to make, and even better after a short rest in the fridge.

### Ingredients:

  * 3–4 dl Greek yoghurt

  * ½ cucumber, grated

  * 1–2 cloves garlic, finely grated or pressed

  * Salt and pepper

  * Optional: a pinch of toasted, crushed cumin

  * Optional: a little fresh mint, finely chopped

### Steps:

  * Grate the cucumber, salt lightly and let it drain in a colander for 10 minutes. Squeeze out the excess liquid — this is what decides whether the raita turns out creamy or watery.

  * Mix the yoghurt, drained cucumber and garlic. Season with salt, pepper, and the cumin and mint if using.

  * Chill for at least 20–30 minutes before serving, longer if you can.

  * Best made the same day — the cucumber releases liquid over time, so avoid making it the night before.
