A simple, filling salad that pairs perfectly with grilled food. Takes 5 minutes, and everyone likes it.

### Ingredients:

  * 4–5 tomatoes, chopped

  * 1 cucumber, chopped

  * 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

  * 150 gr feta cheese, cubed or crumbled

  * A handful of olives (optional)

  * 3 tbs olive oil

  * Juice of 1/2 lemon

  * Salt and pepper

  * Fresh oregano or basil (optional)

### Steps:

  * Mix tomato, cucumber and red onion in a large bowl.

  * Add feta cheese and olives.

  * Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.

  * Serve immediately.
